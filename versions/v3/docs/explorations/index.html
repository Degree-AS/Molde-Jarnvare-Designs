Visual direction — alternatives to v2

Feedback on v2: "boring", "old-fashioned", "outdated". Two alternative directions that aim for a fresher, more modern feel while still representing Molde Jarnvare's industrial tradition. Each is a complete, self-contained home page mock-up — click to open in full.

Direction A

Editorial Heritage

Warm cream backdrop, a confident serif display face, a giant "1930" as graphic anchor, and terracotta in place of pure orange. Feels like a trusted craft partner — modern but rooted.

  • Fraunces serif (display) + Source Sans 3 (body)
  • Cream + deep navy + terracotta
  • Pill buttons, rounded corners, paper textures
  • Magazine-grid people cards, editorial product cards

Direction B

Industrial Modern

Dark-mode hero with high contrast, a tight geometric sans-serif, live lager-data ("30 247 artikler på lager nå"), and a vivid safety-orange accent. Feels like a confident modern industrial brand.

  • Space Grotesk (display) + Inter (body) + JetBrains Mono (data)
  • Graphite black + cool white + safety orange
  • Crisp 4-6 px corners, structural lines, mono detail
  • "Live" data panel, // section markers, accent flags on products
